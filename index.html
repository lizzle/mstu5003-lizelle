Professional Experience

I've always been interested in interdisciplinary education. As a child, I dreamed of a career that combined writing and art, and in the past few years, I've added education and technology to that list.

I received my B.A. in English from UC Berkeley, where I assisted with research on computer-based formative assessment with the Graduate School of Education. Since then, I've worked with audiences ranging from K-8 students in low-income communities to executives at Fortune 500 companies.

Currently, I'm finishing my Master's degree at Teachers College, Columbia University, where my interests include designing games, curriculum, EdTech tools and experiences.
